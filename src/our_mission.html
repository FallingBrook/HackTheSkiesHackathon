Eco Vision

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

How It Started

Our eco-friendly garbage bin project was born at the Hack the Skies hackathon, where we were

challenged to create a solution that addresses a pressing global issue. Inspired by the growing

environmental crisis and the inefficiencies in traditional waste management systems, our team came

together with a shared vision to leverage AI technology for a sustainable future. The hackathon

provided the perfect platform to turn this idea into a prototype.

Our Goal

We aim to redefine how waste is managed by integrating intelligent sorting technology into

everyday practices. Our goal is to make recycling accessible, accurate, and effortless, helping

individuals and communities reduce waste and contribute to a cleaner planet. Through innovation

and collaboration, we strive to create lasting environmental impact.
