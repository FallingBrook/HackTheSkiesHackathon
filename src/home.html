Our Latest Innovation: Eco Lens

Eco Lens is an innovative, AI-powered garbage bin designed to make waste management smarter

and more sustainable. Using advanced image recognition technology, Eco Lens identifies whether an

item is recyclable or non-recyclable and sorts it into the correct compartment automatically. By

simplifying the recycling process, Eco Lens empowers individuals and communities to reduce waste,

minimize environmental impact, and contribute to a greener future.
